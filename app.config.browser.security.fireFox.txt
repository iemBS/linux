Config FireFox Web Browser Security
Main Success Scenario: 
  1. Open FireFox browser
  2. Click menu on top right 
  3. Choose "Options"
  4. Choose "Privacy & Security" (lock symbol)
  5. For "Content Blocking", choose "Strict"
  6. Choose "Always" for "Send websites a “Do Not Track” signal that you don’t want to be tracked"
  7. Choose "Never remember history" for "History"
  8. For "Permissions"
       - Location > check "Block new requests asking to access your location"
       - Camera > check "Block new requests asking to access your camera"
       - Microphone > check "Block new requests asking to access your microphone"
       - check "Block pop-up windows"
       - check "Warn you when websites try to install add-ons"
  9. For "Security", check "Block dangerous and deceptive content"
  10. For "Certificates"
        - choose "Ask you every time"
        - check "Query OCSP responder servers to confirm the current validity of certificates"
