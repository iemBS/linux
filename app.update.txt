Update Software
Main Success Scenario:
  1. Open up a terminal window.
  2. Run "sudo apt-get upgrade" in terminal window.
  3. Enter password.
  4. Look over the list of available updates and decide if you want to go through with the entire upgrade.
  5. To accept all updates click the 'y' key (no quotes) and hit Enter.
  6. Watch as the update happens.
